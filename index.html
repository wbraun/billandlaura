Travel

Plane

Cozumel International Airport (CZM) is the primary airport servicing Cozumel, an island which is approximately 30 miles tall, 10 miles wide and just 10 miles east of continental Mexico. There are direct flights from Bush (IAH) via United Airlines and Hobby (HOU) via Southwest Airlines. There are also flights from Dallas (DFW) which may be useful for connecting flights.

There is plenty to do in Cozumel. It's most famous for world class snorkeling and scuba diving in the world’s second-largest reef with abundant and colorful marine life. There are the usual beach activities, sunset boat tours, dolphin swims, horseback riding and an ecological park. Cozumel also offers tours to many of the Mayan ruin sites, both in Cozumel and on the mainland.
